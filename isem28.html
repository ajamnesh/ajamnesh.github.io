Together with Henrik Kreidler, I organized the 28th Edition of the Internet Seminar (ISem) in the academic year 2024/25. The topic of ISem28 was "Ergodic Structure Theory and Applications".

The Internet Seminar is an annual, year-long international mathematical program aimed at Master’s and PhD students, consisting of three stages: a lecture phase, a project phase, and a final workshop. Each year, it explores advanced topics in analysis and related areas. For an overview of previous editions, see this page.

In February 2025, we successfully concluded the virtual lecture phase of ISem28 with over 400 participants organized into international local groups. The lecture phase culminated in a set of self-contained lecture notes (250 pages), which you can download here: ISem28 Lecture Notes.

The project phase comprised 11 international project groups coordinated by independent project leaders. These groups presented their results at the final workshop, which took place July 14–18, 2025, in Wuppertal. Below you can find some impressions from the final workshop.
