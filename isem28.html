Together with Henrik Kreidler, I am organizing the 28th Edition of the Internet Seminar (ISem). The topic of ISem28 is "Ergodic Structure Theory and Applications".

The Internet Seminar is an annual, year-long international mathematical program aimed at Master’s and PhD students. Each year, it explores advanced topics in analysis and related areas. For an overview of previous editions, see this page.

ISem consists of three stages: a lecture phase, a project phase, and a final workshop. In February 2025, we successfully concluded the virtual lecture phase of ISem28 with over 400 participants organized into international local groups.

The lecture phase culminated in a set of self-contained lecture notes (250 pages), which you can download here: ISem28 Lecture Notes.

We are currently in the project phase, comprising 11 project groups coordinated by independent project leaders. These groups will present their results at the final workshop, taking place July 14–18, 2025, in Wuppertal.

For more details, visit the official ISem28 website.
